Superstore Sales Dashboard & Forecast (Power BI Project)
- Project Overview

This Power BI project provides a comprehensive view of Superstore sales performance and future sales forecast.
It helps analyze key performance indicators like total sales, profit, quantity, and delivery trends while uncovering insights by region, category, payment mode, and customer segment.
Additionally, a 15-day sales forecast has been created using Power BI’s built-in analytics tools.

- Key Metrics
Metric	Value
Total Sales	1.6M
Total Quantity Sold	22K
Total Profit	175K
Average Delivery Days	4

Tool: Microsoft Power BI

- Dashboard Insights
-- Sales by Category & Subcategory

Office Supplies leads with 0.64M sales, followed by Technology (0.47M) and Furniture (0.45M).

Within subcategories, Phones (0.20M) generate the highest sales.

-- Sales by Region

West Region (33%) contributes the most, followed by East (28%) and Central (22%).

South (16%) has potential for growth.

-- Sales by Payment Mode

Cash on Delivery (43%) is the most used mode, followed by Online Payments (35%) and Cards (22%).

-- Sales by Ship Mode

Standard Class (0.91M) dominates shipments — efficient logistics channel.

Same-day deliveries remain minimal at 0.10M.

-- Monthly Sales YoY Trend

Noticeable sales growth from August to December, especially in 2020.

Seasonal demand spikes near year-end festivals.

-- Sales by Segment

Consumer Segment (48%) drives almost half of total sales.

Corporate (33%) and Home Office (19%) follow.

- Forecast Analysis

The Sales Forecast Dashboard predicts the next 15 days of performance using Power BI’s forecasting model on time-series data.

Expected sales trend indicates stable growth with minor dips.

Forecast range (highlighted in pink) provides upper and lower confidence intervals for prediction accuracy.

- Business Insights

Strong opportunity in South Region and Corporate Segment for expansion.

Phones and Technology categories should be prioritized for inventory and marketing.

Optimize shipping methods — standard class performs best, but same-day delivery can be expanded for urban zones.

Leverage forecast data for better demand planning and inventory management.
